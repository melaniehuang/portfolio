Adidas China

Innovation Lab

It was time to renovate and invigorate the retail experience!

Boost shoe technology was always highly regarded as revolutionary running technology however, with the release of Boost 2.0 - Adidas China not only wanted to celebrate the new and improved foam but open the market up to runners of all skill levels.

An interactive retail experience like no other

The innovaton lab featured five retail touchpoints: Interactive treadmill station, Boost pad interactives, multi-touch screens, shoe hologram and the front call-to-innovation-lab screen.

Boost interactive touch screen

In terms of stay time, we knew the touch screen was probably going to draw in the longest interaction time with the Adidas brand so we wanted to create an engaging and informative experience. Heroing the shoe recommender, a large body of work was completed to ensure that not only was the user experience of content flowing nicely but the shoe recommender was helpful to the customer.
