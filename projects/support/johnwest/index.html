The Amazing Race Australia vs. New Zealand app

Ten countries, six continents and almost 90,000kms without leaving your lounge room.

When it comes to reality tv shows, everyone thinks they can do one better! So we extended the opportunity to race around the world to our home viewers but from the comfort of their lounge room.

Every week a new challenge!

In sync with the weekly broadcast and being a game of chance and prizes, we worked alongside The Amazing Race and Channel 7 to ensure we had all our bases covered when it came to both sign ups and game releases.

10 mini-games

Every game released was based on a challenge featured in that weeks' Amazing Race episode. Whether it be to find the sheep with the clue or five hidden monks in the scenery, the objective was always accuracy and efficiency.
