The Park Lane HK x Paul Kolp

Kaleidoscopic Foyer

Sensual, textural and all-consuming - a hotel lobby transformed by the art inside itself.

In collaboration with grain&pixel and Galerie Junger (Berlin - Shanghai), we wanted the boutique hotel to reflect its essence: art. Set to magnify Kolp's unique world of colour and texture, we created a giant kaleidoscope of Kolp's photography bringing out Kolp's iconic and distinct use of texture in his work.
