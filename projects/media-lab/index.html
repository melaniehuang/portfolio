Media Lab Melbourne

Building a community

Media Lab Melbourne is a not-for-profit dedicated to bringing together creators from all fields of practise to explore the potential of new technologies.

As part of the Media Lab Melbourne committee, I help organise a meetup on creative technology called DemoLAB. In addition to this, I helped rebrand and redesign Media Lab Melbourne’s website and identity to give a fresh face to an iconic body. With a rich history in building communities around creative technology, we are working on more events and workshops for 2018.
