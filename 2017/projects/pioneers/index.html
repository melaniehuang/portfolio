The Pioneers of Computer Science

Visualising achievement

The Pioneers of Computer Science is an interactive visualisation of the minds behind some of our greatest milestones in technology.

Starting as a personal research topic whilst travelling, developer Andrew Buntine decided to collect and present data on pioneers that he admired. Transforming into a full blown database of the "who" and "when" for TOO many topics in the field, I helped with the design of the site however, full credit goes to Andrew on the enormous effort in hand collecting and presenting the data.
