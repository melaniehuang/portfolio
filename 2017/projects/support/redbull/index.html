Notions of Sonic Space

A world of beats, bass and imagination on giant gramophones!

Suspended above the Forum Theatre stage in an auditorium decored with Roman imagination, loomed three metre tall grammophones ready and waiting for the night ahead. In an interactive stage design that only Eness could've pulled off, I had the absolute pleasure of working alongside the guys from sourcing video content to constructing and drilling the gramophones.
