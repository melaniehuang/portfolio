Unlock your Style

Generative Autumn Winter styling

For the launch of the new season looks, Highpoint set out to do something a little different.

Virtual closet, real items and trends to match

Partnering with over 20 fashion labels, Highpoint put the power in their shoppers hands to discover what style they should be looking out for this Autumn/Winter. The live style board, coupled with generative animated trend titles and personalised flatlays, Unlock your Style was brought to life online and in centre.
