Media Lab Melbourne is a not-for-profit dedicated to bringing together creators from all fields of practise to explore the potential of new technologies. 

From meetups to workshops, exhibitions to collaborations — we’re dedicated to promoting artists, designers, scientists, engineers, architects, developers, couturiers, performers and every imagination in between to come together and make great things.
