Disposable x Science Gallery
p@tch by Janna Ahrndt

About

P@tch is a textile-based new media project, inspired by punk and DIY culture, by Janna Ahrndt.

Each homemade P@tch uses self-reporting and environmental sensors to allow the user to track their personal and surrounding pollution.

Science Gallery Melbourne commissioned this data to be aggregated and visualised publicly throughout the month of August.
