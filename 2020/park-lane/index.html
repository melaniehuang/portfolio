The Park Lane x grain&pixel
Kaleidoscopic Foyer

About

Sensual, textural and all-consuming - we transformed the hotel lobby with the art inside itself.

We wanted the boutique hotel to reflect its essence: art. Set to magnify Kolp's unique world of colour and texture, we created a giant kaleidoscope, set on a 9.5 meter media wall. The effect was Kolp's sensual textures reflecting 360 on every surface to completely immerse the visitor on arrival.
