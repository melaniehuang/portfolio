SYSTEM_ERROR

About

“SYSTEM_ERROR contemplates our shared disquiet around the frailties and imperfections that make us human, and interrogates the increasingly dependent and integrated relationship between human bodies, brains and technology.” - Chamber Made

Currently a work in progress, we are exploring the use of the human body to perform and express the sound and visuals of the performance through a bespoke electronic instrument with conductive contact points that can measure and read human contact and pressure in real time.
