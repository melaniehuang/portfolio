About

Every Technecolour workshop is unique in content but consistent in structure. Our workshops explore the world of coding creatively, framing it as a tool to inspire our image making.

We are here to inspire and be inspired by the organisations and institutions we partner with and are passionate about making fun and engaging content that is relevant to our participants.

We have a wonderful team of industry professionals on hand to help and most importantly, we promise an open and inclusive learning environment for all ages, technical abilities and learning styles.

To learn more, visit http://technecolour.org/

Credits

All workshops are written and taught by Mel Huang with the help of a wonderful community of designers, developers and makers.

Workshops have been hosted in partnership with Linden New Art, Signal, Code like a Girl, Melbourne Knowledge Week, Melbourne Design Week, Processing Community Day Aus/NZ and City of Melbourne.
