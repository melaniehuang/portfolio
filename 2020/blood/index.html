Blood x Science Gallery
Preserving indigenous language

About

“The original inhabitants of Australia, Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples, belonged to over 700 different nations….Fewer than 150 of the languages remain in regular use and all but a few are now considered endangered.”

Science Gallery commissioned Gurrk for their first inaugural exhibition 'Blood'. As part of this artwork, I designed and built the front-end for an interactive map with translations and sound for the word ‘blood’ in language.

Credits

In collaboration with Research Unit for Indigenous Language and The University of Melbourne Digital Humanities team

As part of Blood: Attract and Repel at Science Gallery Melbourne

2 Aug – 23 Sept 2017
