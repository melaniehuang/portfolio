Art Gallery NSW
Japan Supernatural

About

A wildly imaginative exhibition exploring the fascinating world of Japanese folklore. Working with the idea of breath and twilight, we gave life into the exhibition’s motif of the moon amist an indigo blue sky.

The audio experience features an original musical score composed and arranged by Natural Progression Studios in collaboration with Japanese vocalist and virtuoso master of the Japanese shamisen, Noriko Tadano, and Toshi Sakamoto of renowned Japanese Taiko drumming ensemble Wadaiko Rindo.

The experience was available in both English and Japanese with over 55,000 listens and an average listening time of 62 minutes.
